]]> Modern graphics and advertisements by many large-scale industries through popular influencers lured us into a trap of blind faith and trust, that made us forget about our origin and methods used by our ancestors to do the job.

Advancements and growth are better but, as a responsible consumer, you should verify what you are consuming! In most households, I barely noticed anyone concerned about toxicity in modern cleaning products.

Why should I worry about toxicity in cleaning products?

1. Potential cause of Cancer and other diseases

By using toxic cleansers, you are exposing your body to harmful chemicals in many ways such as direct contact with skin or inhaling vapors during cleaning.

If you do laundry, the detergent may remain in your cloth after the wash cycle is complete and has the potential to get absorbed through the skin. It may even cause allergies!

If you wash your dishes with toxic gel, just imagine how many times you are consuming chemicals with food or beverage!

Every time, when you clean the toilet with a harmful, yet powerful cleanser, you are inhaling toxic fumes from the toilet bowl.

2. Non-Biodegradable

Water waste is generated every time we do laundry, dish wash, or clean the toilet. Such harmful products may contain dye, artificial fragrance, color, and other synthetic chemicals, which cannot be broken down by microbes, and thus, they are non-biodegradable products!

Toxic chemicals from many household activities are exploiting the environment and imagine the level of abuse to nature by humans!
